Insurance Guide

INSURANCE TYPE
LIFE INSURANCE
WHO NEEDS THIS?
People with dependents (spouse, children, elderly parents), significant debt, or a desire to leave a legacy.
WHEN DO THEY NEED IT?
Anytime, but especially when you have dependents or significant financial obligations.
INSURANCE TYPE
HEALTH INSURANCE
WHO NEEDS THIS?
Virtually everyone, as unexpected medical expenses can be devastating.
WHEN DO THEY NEED IT?
As soon as possible after starting a new job or losing previous coverage.
INSURANCE TYPE
LONG-TERM DISABILITY INSURANCE
WHO NEEDS THIS?
Anyone who relies on their income for living expenses, especially those with high-earning potential.
WHEN DO THEY NEED IT?
Before a disabling event occurs, preferably while you're young and healthy.
INSURANCE TYPE
LONG-TERM CARE INSURANCE
WHO NEEDS THIS?
Individuals who may need assistance with daily living activities in the future.
WHEN DO THEY NEED IT?
Before the need arises, as premiums increase with age.
INSURANCE TYPE
CAR/AUTO INSURANCE
WHO NEEDS THIS?
Anyone who owns or operates a vehicle. More important for those with newer cars that are worth more money.
WHEN DO THEY NEED IT?
As soon as you purchase a vehicle or start driving it.
INSURANCE TYPE
PROPERTY/HOME INSURANCE
WHO NEEDS THIS?
Homeowners and landlords. Helps keep your stuff and property insured and safe in case of property damage.
WHEN DO THEY NEED IT?
When you purchase a home or become a landlord.
INSURANCE TYPE
RENTER'S INSURANCE
WHO NEEDS THIS?
Example: Anyone renting an apartment or a storage unit. Sometimes when renting a car (check your auto insurance policy).
WHEN DO THEY NEED IT?
Once you start the rental period, you should get the insurance - even while moving in. You can cancel it once you're completely moved out/done.
