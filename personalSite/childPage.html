Welcome to our personalized strength estimation tool! By providing your gender, weight, height, and a self-assessment of muscle density, we'll calculate your BMI and offer a general range of lifting expectations for bench press, deadlifts, and squats. Please remember that these are estimations based on averages and individual results may vary. This tool is designed to provide a starting point for your fitness journey, offering a realistic perspective on potential strength development. For personalized guidance and to ensure safe lifting practices, consulting with a certified fitness professional is always recommended.
